Delivery Logistics for Building Materials: The Dispatch Checklist

Updated: 2026-02-17 • Back to blog

Avoid site stoppage with a dispatch checklist: truck size, access, unloading method, and timing.

Truck-fit

Choose vehicle by road width, turning radius, and unloading points.

Unloading

Crane/manual/forklift — confirm before dispatch.

Proof

Capture time-out/time-in, km run, and recipient signature for disputes.

Quick buying checklist

  • Confirm spec (grade/standard/application), not just the brand name.
  • Normalize units (per bag/kg/sqm/litre). Catch 10x errors early.
  • Plan delivery: access, unloading method, time window.
  • Keep documents: quote/PO to invoice to delivery proof.
