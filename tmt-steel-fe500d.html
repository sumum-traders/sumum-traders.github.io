TMT Steel Bars: Why Fe 500D Matters (Ductility, Earthquake, Bending)

Updated: 2026-02-17 • Back to blog

How to select TMT grade and mill test certificates for structural safety.

Fe 500 vs Fe 500D

'D' indicates higher ductility requirements; improves energy absorption in seismic loads.

What to ask

Brand/grade, size, batch/heat number, mill TC, BIS marking, bend/re-bend performance.

Site reality

Storage and rust prevention matter; uncontrolled corrosion destroys effective diameter.

Quick buying checklist

  • Confirm spec (grade/standard/application), not just the brand name.
  • Normalize units (per bag/kg/sqm/litre). Catch 10x errors early.
  • Plan delivery: access, unloading method, time window.
  • Keep documents: quote/PO to invoice to delivery proof.
