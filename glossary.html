Glossary

Glossary of common building materials, abbreviations, and standards used in quotes and procurement.

TermMeaning
OPC / PPC / PSCCement types: Ordinary Portland Cement / Portland Pozzolana Cement / Portland Slag Cement.
Fe 500DDuctile grade of TMT steel as per BIS requirements.
C1 / C2 / S1Adhesive classes indicating adhesion and flexibility.
SBRStyrene-butadiene rubber latex used as bonding/waterproofing additive.
RCCB / MCB / SPDElectrical protection devices: residual current, miniature breaker, surge protection device.
SWRSoil-Waste-Rainwater pipe category for drainage.
BOQBill of Quantities; itemized material and work list for a project.
GRNGoods Receipt Note; receiving record for matching invoice and inventory.
MTCMill Test Certificate for steel; batch/heat verification.
Pond testWater retention test used to validate waterproofing in wet areas.
