Procurement Checklist: BOQ to Purchase Order (Reduce Variance)

Updated: 2026-02-17 • Back to blog

A systems checklist to reduce variance and leakage: spec lock, vendor selection, and approval controls.

Spec lock

Freeze grade/brand/standard before negotiating rates.

Compare apples

Normalize unit: per bag, per kg, per sqm, per litre; watch hidden 10x errors.

Approvals

Separate quote creation from release approval (two-person control).

Quick buying checklist

  • Confirm spec (grade/standard/application), not just the brand name.
  • Normalize units (per bag/kg/sqm/litre). Catch 10x errors early.
  • Plan delivery: access, unloading method, time window.
  • Keep documents: quote/PO to invoice to delivery proof.
