Blog: long-form buying guides

The blog carries eleven long-form articles that walk a buyer through the specification, purchase and site-delivery of each major product category stocked at the Kyathsandra yard. The articles are written for three audiences in parallel: a homeowner finishing a single house, a contractor or site engineer procuring for an active project, and an institutional or project buyer running a multi-line tender with formal documentation. Each article is cross-linked to the relevant category landing page and to the corresponding technical library article.

Reading order suggestions

A homeowner building a new house can read the articles in the order that matches the construction sequence, beginning with cement grades for foundations and columns, moving to TMT steel for reinforcement, then ready-mix versus site-mix for the structural pours, then waterproofing for the plinth, then tile adhesive for flooring, then paint and putty for finishing, and closing with plumbing pipes and electrical wiring for the services fit-out. A contractor preparing a commercial bid can read the procurement checklist, the GST invoice guide and the delivery logistics article together as the commercial backbone of the tender response, then dip into the category-specific articles as the bill of quantities demands. An institutional buyer running a formal tender can begin with the procurement checklist, cross-refer the invoicing and logistics articles, and then delegate the category-level reading to the technical team.
