Tile Adhesive and Grout: C1/C2/S1 Explained for Indian Tiles

Updated: 2026-02-17 • Back to blog

Pick adhesives and grouts by tile size, substrate movement, wet areas, and installation speed.

C1 vs C2

C2 has higher adhesion; better for vitrified/large format and exterior.

Flex S1

Use when substrate moves: balconies, terraces, gypsum boards, thermal swings.

Grout

Epoxy grout for stain resistance; cement grout for standard dry areas.

Quick buying checklist

  • Confirm spec (grade/standard/application), not just the brand name.
  • Normalize units (per bag/kg/sqm/litre). Catch 10x errors early.
  • Plan delivery: access, unloading method, time window.
  • Keep documents: quote/PO to invoice to delivery proof.
