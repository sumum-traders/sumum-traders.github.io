Electrical Essentials: Conduit, Wire Sizing, and Panel Protection

Updated: 2026-02-17 • Back to blog

A simple guide to conduits, wires, and protective devices to reduce fire risk and nuisance trips.

Conduits

Use correct diameter; avoid overfill; plan routes to reduce bends and pull issues.

Protection stack

Isolator to SPD to RCCB to MCBs; earthing matters as much as device brand.

Quality markers

ISI marking, copper purity, proper lugging and termination torque.

Quick buying checklist

  • Confirm spec (grade/standard/application), not just the brand name.
  • Normalize units (per bag/kg/sqm/litre). Catch 10x errors early.
  • Plan delivery: access, unloading method, time window.
  • Keep documents: quote/PO to invoice to delivery proof.
