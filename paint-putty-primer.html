Paint + Putty + Primer: The Correct Order (and Where Projects Waste Money)

Updated: 2026-02-17 • Back to blog

Sequence and selection guide for primers, putty, and paints to reduce peeling, patchiness, and rework.

Correct order

Surface prep to primer to putty (if needed) to primer sealer to paint coats.

Common waste

Skipping primer or over-thinning paint; rework cost is often 10x.

Exterior logic

UV + rain need better systems: exterior primer + exterior paint; add waterproofing where required.

Quick buying checklist

  • Confirm spec (grade/standard/application), not just the brand name.
  • Normalize units (per bag/kg/sqm/litre). Catch 10x errors early.
  • Plan delivery: access, unloading method, time window.
  • Keep documents: quote/PO to invoice to delivery proof.
