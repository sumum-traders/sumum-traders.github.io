RMC vs Site Mix: When Ready-Mix Actually Saves Money

Updated: 2026-02-17 • Back to blog

A cost + quality comparison: cement theft, water ratio drift, and rework risk.

Hidden costs

Site mix variance causes cracks/rework — often a 10x cost vs small RMC premium.

When RMC wins

Large pours, tight deadlines, QC needs, limited supervision.

When site mix is fine

Small volume, strong supervision, reliable batching discipline.

Quick buying checklist

  • Confirm spec (grade/standard/application), not just the brand name.
  • Normalize units (per bag/kg/sqm/litre). Catch 10x errors early.
  • Plan delivery: access, unloading method, time window.
  • Keep documents: quote/PO to invoice to delivery proof.
