Sumum Traders LLP - Building Materials Supply, South India

Sumum Traders LLP is a Karnataka-registered Limited Liability Partnership
(LLPIN AAE-3987) trading in building materials. The firm operates from
a single yard and office at Kyathsandra in Tumakuru and despatches
material to project sites across 26 South Indian cities.

Categories stocked
- Cement and binders: OPC 43, OPC 53, PPC, PSC, white cement, putty
- Steel and rebar: Fe 500, Fe 500 D, Fe 550 D TMT, structural sections
- Tiles and stone: vitrified, ceramic, GVT, double-charge, marble
- Paints and coatings: emulsions, enamels, wood and metal finishes
- Waterproofing: cementitious, acrylic, polyurethane, bitumen membrane
- Adhesives and chemicals: tile adhesives, grouts, admixtures
- Pipes and plumbing: CPVC, uPVC, SWR, PPR, sanitaryware
- Electrical and lighting: MCBs, RCCBs, wires, switches, luminaires
